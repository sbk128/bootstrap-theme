About Us

YuvaLeads is an organisation created and directed by a group of enthusiasts to create a strong, vibrant, intelligent student community locally, and nationally. YuvaLeads is envisioned as a platform to provide opportunities to the students and to enable them to explore their strengths and know themselves and their interests, ambitions and give them a clearer picture of the real side of the industry and leadership. We propose to take a two-way approach to create this strong community of the students. Under the YUVALEADS banners we are creating the ILead initiative, where our objective is to take the student interns and volunteers through the various stages of leadership and soft skill development to prepare them for a better career ahead.

Our Mission Our Vision Various Ideas Two Way Approach Implementation Plan

Our Mission

  • Building a strong Youth community, which is focused towards making positive contributions to the growth of the individual, the community and the nation.
  • Creating a more thoughtful and deeply engaged class of youth who would be willing to be the change agent and be far more empathetic towards the social and political climate of our country.
  • Influence the government to involve more youth and create youth centric policies for the economic development of our country.
  • Create a talented pool of professionals who have the initiative taking ability to guide the country into creating and leading corporate giants.

Our Vision

  • Training the Youth to take leadership roles at Social, Political and Corporate levels for creating a New Bharat.
  • Engaging the student community in large to help them understand the gaps in their education.
  • Creating skill programmes and workshops to fill in the discovered gaps in the system.
  • Provide youngsters with a platform to discover their hidden potential and help them understand if that can be taken up for profession.
  • Create and conduct workshops / trips and programmes around the culture and history of our country to help them get acquainted with the past.
  • Provide a platform to young startups to explore their idea and pursue it fearlessly (yuvastarts).
  • Enable the youth from tier 2 and tier 3 cities to implement their ideas into money earning opportunities.

Two-Way Approach

Top Down Approach

We organize events with One College as the Hub. – Right now Atharva Institute of Management Studies.

Bottom Up Approach

We appoint one student representative at each college He has the job of bringing in 50 volunteers or participants from his college / institute / organization for the event and conduct 2 events at that level per year. On successfully doing this he is eligible for the title of a community builder. We create a core team of builders at area level, the head of this is the community manager.

Our Implementation Plan

Step 1:
We aim to begin with leadership sessions and talks by various eminent personalities who are thought and action leaders in their own domain. We are already conducting leadership sessions at the Atharva Institute of Management Studies
Step 2:
We will start conducting problem/issue/skill specific workshops for the students either free of cost or for an affordable price depending on various factors.
Step 3:
We will create volunteering opportunities with various social enterprises and internship opportunities with our corporate partners (including startups, MNC, SMEs and government bodies and think tanks).
